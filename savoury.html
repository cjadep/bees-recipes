Savoury Tart

Ingredients

  • 5x thick slices of bread
  • 1x tbls sugar
  • 1x cup milk
  • 1/2x tps baking powder
  • 3x eggs
  • 1x pinch of salt
  • 1x cup chopped ham or tin salmon or bacon bits(optional)
  • 1x chopped onion and/or asparagus(optional)
  • 1 1/2 cups of grated cheddar cheese(optional)
  • 1x tbls chopped chives or parsley (optional)

Method

Grease/spray a pie dish or little shallow muffin pans. Cut crusts off the bread and break bread into small pieces. Mix all ingredients well. Place in dish or pans and bake at 180 degrees Celsius or until golden brown +/- 30 mins.

Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 x chicken or giblets and liver
  • 2x large grated carrots
  • 2x large diced carrots
  • 1x bunch of finely chopped parsley
  • 2x tsp of salt
  • 1x chopped onion
  • 3/4x cup of rice

Method

Boil the chicken or giblets for 1 ½ hours, add the rice, salt, diced carrots, and onion. Boil until the carrot is cooked, approximately 30min-45min. Add the parsley and grated carrot and cook for another 15min or until ready.

Potato Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 x piece of shin
  • 2x large diced carrots
  • 2x large diced carrots
  • 1x diced turnip
  • 1x chopped leak
  • 1x bunch of finely chopped parsley
  • 4x sliced potatoes
  • 1 1/2x tsp of salt

Method

Cook piece of shin for 1 hour, then take the meat out and add the carrots, turnip, leak and potatoes. Add in the salt and cook for 45min. Add the grated carrot and cook until ready.

Ham and Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 x piece of ham
  • 1/2x pound of lentils
  • 2x diced carrots
  • 1x diced turnip
  • 1x chopped leak
  • 1x bunch of finely chopped parsley
  • 1x tsp of salt

Method

Boil the joint of ham for about 1 ½ hours, then add the lentils, carrots, turnips, and leak. Simmer for about 30min, then add carrot, parsley, and salt. Boil until ready.

Potato Bake

Ingredients

  • 4x potatoes
  • 1x cup milk
  • 250ml cream
  • 200g grated cheese
  • 4x slices of ham
  • 1/3 tsp garlic (or to taste)

Method

Slice the potatoes thinly and boil in milk until they are soft. Keep the milk. Layer potatoes in a dish and pour the milk and cream over. Season and then repeat the layer. Place the cheese and chopped ham over the top, add some garlic and bake until ready.

Blue Cheese Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 x blue cheese
  • 1x butter (equal to the blue cheese)
  • 1/2x tsp of garlic (or to taste)
  • 250ml cream
  • 2x tsp of Maizina (corn starch)
  • 1/3x tsp of fresh black pepper (or to taste)

Method

Melt the butter and add the garlic, let it simmer a little. Break the blue cheese into the melted butter. When the cheese is melted, add the cream. To thicken the sauce, add the Maizina (mixed with a little milk or sauce to avoid clumping). Add the black pepper and simmer for a while and stir often.
