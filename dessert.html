Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2x Moirs Moist Chocolate Cake mix (substitute available)
  • 1 1/2x cups of milk
  • 1/2x cup of sugar
  • 2 tbls margarine
  • 200g wholenut chocolate

Method

Bake the moirs chocolate cake according to the instructions on the back. Boil the milk,sugar and margarine together. When the cake comes out of the oven, pour this mixture over the hot cake. Melt the chocolate and mix with some milk to make it saucy. Pour the chocolate sauce over the cake when it has cooled.

Caramel Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 x tin of condensed milk
  • 3x eggs
  • 1x tsp vanilla essence
  • 4x tbls sugar
  • 1x tin of water

Method

Beat eggs, condensed milk and water, then add vanilla essence. Melt the sugar and stir until it becomes caramel (till pale brown). Pour in bottom of dish. Pour over caramel. Put the water in a separate dish (1/2 full) and place in the oven for 1 hour at 160 degrees Celsius. Let cool, then place in the fridge for 3 hours. Put cream on top with flake.

Pineapple Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1x packet of pineapple jelly
  • 200ml boiling water
  • 1x 410g tin of pineapple pieces
  • 250ml fresh cream
  • 250g cream cheese
  • 1x packet of tennis biscuits

Method

Drain the syrup off the pineapple pieces and retain 150ml. Dissolve the jelly powder in 200ml boiling water and then add the 150ml of pineapple syrup. Allow to cool and refrigerate until semi-set. Whip the cream to soft peaks. Fold the whipped cream and the cream cheese into jelly. Spread a layer of this mixture in an oblong dish (ie. 25cm x 16cm). Place a few pineapple pieces on top. Place a layer of tennis biscuits on the pineapple pieces and repeat, ending with pineapple. Refrigerate until firmly set.

Pineapple Flan

Ingredients

  • 3x eggs
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 2x cups of flour
  • 3x tsps baking powder
  • 1x pinch of salt
  • 1/4x pound soft margerine
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • 2x cups water (pineapple mixture)
  • 2x cups sugar (pineapple mixture)
  • 1x grated pineapple (pineapple mixture)
  • 4x tbls of custard

Method

Cream the butter and sugar together. Add the eggs one at a time, beating in between. Sift the dry ingredients together and then add them to the mixture. Add milk unitl the mixture is not too runny and not too firm. Bake at 180 degrees celcius for ~25-30mins. Add the water, sugar and grated pineapple together to create the pineapple mixture. Add 4 tbls custard mixed with a little water to the pineapple mix. Boil until the mix is shiny. Leave to cool and place in the flan tin with cream.

Carrot Cake

Ingredients - cake

  • 2 1/2x cups (625ml) flour
  • 2x (10ml) tsp of baking powder
  • 1 1/2x tsp (7.5ml) bicarbonate of soda
  • 3x tsp (15ml) mixed spice
  • 1x tsp (5ml) salt
  • 1 1/2x cups (375ml) sugar
  • 1 1/4x cups (310ml) of oil
  • 4x eggs
  • 2x cups (500ml) grated carrots/ granny smith apples
  • 1x cup (125ml) grated fresh pineapple or crushed tinned pineapple
  • 1/2x cup (125ml) chopped pecan nuts
  • 1/4x cup (62.5ml) smooth apricot jam
  • 250g raisins (optional)

Method - cake

Sift the dry ingredients together (twice). Beat the sugar, oil and eggs together until well-blended. Use an electric hand beater and beat at high speed for 3 mins. Sift the dry ingredients over the mixture and fold in with a spatula until uniformly blended.

Carrot Cake cont.

Grease a 28cm loose-bottomed springform tube or angel cake tin and sprinkle with dry breadcrumbs to coat lightly. Pour the mixture into the prepared cake tin and bakle at 180-190 degrees celcius for 45 min. Allow the cake to firm and cool in the tin for 10min before turning out.

Ingredients - icing

  • 1/2x cup (125ml) butter
  • 4x (500g) icing sugar
  • 1x tsp (5ml) vanilla essence
  • 125g or 250g of cream cheese

Method - icing

Cream the butter and gradually add the icing sugar. Add the vanilla, followed by the cream cheese. Don't beat the cheese too much as the mixture will become watery. (If you drip dry the cream-cheese in a muslin cloth overnight , it becomes much firmer.) Ice the cake when it has cooled. Note that the cake can also be baked in a muffin tray for only 30mins.

Pear Pudding

Ingredients

  • 2x tbls margerine
  • 1/2x cup of sugar
  • 1/2x tsp of salt
  • 1x cup flour
  • 1x heaped tsp baking powder
  • 3x eggs
  • 1/2x cup of milk
  • 1x tsp vanilla
  • 1x can of pears
  • 250ml cream (sauce)
  • 1/2x cup of sugar (sauce)

Method

Cream the margerine and sugar adding eggs one at a time. Sift the dry ingredients together and add to the mixture. Add the milk and then pour the mixture into a dish. Drain off the pear syrup and lay the sliced pears onto the mixture. Bake for 20-30min until brown. Boil the cream with 1/2 cup sugar then pour this over the pudding and put it back in the oven at 180 degrees celcius.

Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2x cup (125g) margerine
  • 1x cup (250ml) of sugar
  • 1x tsp (5ml) vanilla essence
  • 1x tsp (5ml) baking powder
  • 1/2x cup (125ml) boiling water
  • 2x eggs
  • 4x mashed bananas
  • 2x cups (500ml) cake flour
  • 1x tsp (5ml) bicarbonate of soda

Method

cream the margerine and sugar together. beat in the vanilla essence and eggs one at a time. Add the sifted flour and baking powder. Add the bicarbonate of soda dissolved in the boiling water. Mix very well. Add the finely mashed bananas. Turn into a greased loaf pan and bake at 180 degrees celcius for 1 hour or until baked.

Royal Hot Milk Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 2x eggs
  • 3/4x cup castor sugar
  • 1/8x tsp salt
  • 1/2x cup of milk
  • 1/2x tsp vanilla extract
  • 1x cup flour
  • 2x tsp Royal baking powder
  • 2x level tbls butter

Method

Sift the flour, salt and baking powder 3 times. Beat the eggs adding the sugar gradually. Add the dry ingredients to the egg mixture and fold in well. Pour in hot milk and butter mixture. Beat for 1 minute. Divide into 2 greased pans and bake at 450 degrees farenheight for 12min. Put together with jam or filling when cold. Ice with water icing.

Peppermint Crisp Tart

Ingredients

  • 2x 250ml cream
  • 2x tins of caramel
  • 1x packet of tennis biscuits
  • 2x large peppermint crisp chocolate bars

Method

Beat the cream until very stiff. Add the caramel and mix. Grate the chocolate into the mixture. Put a layer of tennis biscuits in a dish, pour the mixture over it. Grate some extra chocolate on top. Leave in the fridge to set.

Fruit Cake

Ingredients

  • 1x medium tin of fruit
  • 1x cup flour
  • 1x cup milk
  • 10ml baking powder
  • 1x egg
  • 1x cup sugar
  • 1x tsp (5ml) of oil
  • Some cinnamon and sugar

Method

Place fruit in a greased dish and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Place the egg, flour, sugar, milk, and oil in a shaker and give it a quick shake and mix well. Open the shaker and add the baking powder. Seal again and shake 6 times. Unseal slightly to let it breathe and then shake another 6 times. Pour this over the fruit and bake for ~30min.
