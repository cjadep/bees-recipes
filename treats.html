Bee's Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2x cups all purpose flour
  • 1x tsp salt
  • 1x tsp baking soda
  • 2x eggs
  • 2x tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g unslated room temperature butter
  • 3/4x cup granulated sugar (possibly 1x cup or 180g)
  • 3/4 x cup brown sugar (possibly 1x cup or 180g)
  • 2x cups milk chocolate m&ms (can be switched out for alternatives)

Method

Preheat the oven to 170 degree celcius. Cream butter and sugar together. Beat in eggs and vanilla ( as well as milo/nesquick etc). Fold in flour and m&m's. Drop tablespoons of mixture onto a greased baking tray- allow for spreading. Bake for 18-20mins.

Caramel Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 500g butter/margerine
  • 375g sugar
  • 397g tin condensed milk
  • 750g flour
  • 12.5ml baking powder
  • 1x pinch salt
  • 10ml caramel essence

Method

Cream butter and sugar until tight and fluffy. Stir in the condensed milk. Sift dry ingredients into creamed mixture and mix well. Add essence â€“ beat well. Place into biscuit maker press onto baking tray 180 degrees Celsius 12-15min until golden brown.

Mom's Ginger Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2x eggs
  • 1x tin golden syrup
  • 8x cups flour
  • 4x cups sugar
  • 2x tsp ginger
  • 1/2x tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 250g margerine
  • 1/4x cup warm water

Method

Sift the dry ingredients. Melt the margerine, syrup and then add the eggs . Add the dry ingredients to the mixture. Lastly add the bicarbonate of soda, dissolved in 1/4 cup warm water. Make small balls of dough and flatten with a fork. Bake at 180 degrees celcius until brown.

Mississippi Mud Pies

Ingredients

  • 1/2x cup milk (stage 1)
  • 1/2x cup margerine (stage 1)
  • 1/3x cup cocoa (stage 1)
  • 2x cups sugar (stage 1)
  • 3x cups oats (stage 2)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter (stage 2)
  • 1/4 tsp salt (stage 2)
  • 1x tsp vanilla essence (stage 2)

Method

Boil the stage 1 ingredients together for 1 min. Then remove from the stove and add the stage 2 ingredients. Place tablespoon size portions on a baking tray with wax paper and place in fridge to set.

Boost bars

Ingredients

  • 4x cups oats
  • 4x cups rice cereal
  • 1x cup seedless raisins
  • 1x cup sunflower and pumpkin seeds (chopped)
  • 1x cup chocolate chips
  • 2x cups maple syrup
  • 1 1/2x cups peanut butter (smooth)
  • salt to taste

Method

Heat syrup and peanut butter. Combine remaining ingredients in a large bowl. Stir in the syrupand peanut butter. Pour into a large square pan and let cool.

Belgium Waffles ala Bernice

Ingredients

  • 2x cups flour
  • 4x tsp baking powder
  • 1/4x cup sugar
  • 1/4x tsp salt
  • 2x eggs whites
  • 2x cups milk
  • 1x cup oil
  • 2x egg yolks

Method

Mix together the flour, baking powder, sugar and salt. Whisk the egg whites. In a seperate bowl mix the milk, oil and egg yolks. Mix this with the dry ingredients and fold in the egg whites. Use waffle iron to cook.

Ada's Scones

Ingredients

  • 2x cups flour
  • 1/2x cup oil
  • 1x tsp salt
  • 1/2x cup cheddar cheese
  • 4x tsp baking powder
  • 1x egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 4 1/2 tbls butter

Method

Sift the dry ingredients together. Grate the butter into dry ingredients. Mix thoroughly with your hands. Add the cheese, milk and eggs. Roll out ~3/4 inch thick. Press out with a cookie press. Arrange tightly in a greased baking baking tray. Bake in the oven at 180 degrees celcius for ~15min.

Soet Koekies

Ingredients

  • 12x cups flour
  • 2x pounds margerine
  • 8x tsps baking powder
  • 4x tbls cinnamon
  • 3x tbls cloves
  • 5x cups sugar
  • 1x tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup brandy
  • 4x tbls mixed spice
  • 3x tbls nutmeg
  • 6x eggs

Method

Mix dry ingredients together. Add margerine to form fine crumbs. Add the brandy and eggs. Press out on a floured board and cut to size using a cookie cutter. Before baking brush the biscuits with a little milk and egg mix. Bake at 180 degrees celcius until brown ~ 1-2 hours.
